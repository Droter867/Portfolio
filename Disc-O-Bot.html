High-School Master Thesis

The Disc-O-Bot: Floppydrive music

This is a very old project that I did back in 2015 as my High School Thesis. It is definitely one of my more fun and silly projects that I have made. Basicly I was inspired by this when I came across a video of someone playing "Imperical March" from Star Wars on 2 floopydrives. He had programed the stepper-motors in the floppydrives to turn with specific frequencies that created a pitch.

This mechanical sound was so facinating to me at the time that I thought that it would be fun to try it out for myself. So I bought some old floppydrives and started programing for my first time ever and managed to create a program that reads MIDI-music and transfers it to mechanical signals that perfectly creates the melody of the song.

Unfortunately I dont have any good video footage of the project but here you can find the video that inspired me and give you a ruff understanding of what my Disc-O-Bot sounded like.
