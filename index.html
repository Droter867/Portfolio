About the program

Interaction Technology and Design is a master in computer science engineering that focuses on not only the logical part of software development but the user experience and functionality aswell. Words such as UX (User Exprience) is brought up alot through out the program. A student in Interaction Technology and Design implements and creates applications and software with the user in mind. To ensure a smart and convenient experience. UX-Designers are the future for IT because of their way of looking at code in a whole new perspective.

If we are going to keep making technology mainstream and accesible to ordinary people, we need engineers that knows not only how to write code, but also have a deep knowledge into cognitive science and can design smart and user friendly interfaces as well. And that is what the Interaction Technology and Design program at Umeå University is all about.
