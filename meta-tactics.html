TFT Meta Tactics Helper

Meta Tactics Helper is a webbased application that has been created to help players of the game Teamfight Tactics by giving them a better understanding by provided deep level information about the game and its strategies.

Teamfight Tactics is a round-based game that puts 8 players against each other in a free for fall match. In the game you build a team of champions that fights for you. To win the game you have to be the last one standing of all 8 teams.

Every champion have Traits that can be combined for different effects to your team. A champion can also have up to three items which further increases its power. Meta-Tactics Helper exists to help gather information about every champion, item and traits in one place to show what the best compositions of these are to give the players an edge over their opponents.
