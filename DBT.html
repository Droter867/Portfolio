Design-Build-Test

Food delivery application

This application was developed in partnership with THNX Innovation on the course Design-Build-Test and was a proof of concept about a new kind of white-label food delivery app. The purpose of this app is to give several restuarants their own tailored web application that they can use to deliver food to their customers right to their door.

The application was prototyped in Adobe Xd and later implemented using React.

A link to the prototype can be found here:

https://xd.adobe.com/view/prototype
